About Bharat Bhushan Singla

Senior Advocate · MSMED Panel Advocate · LIC Panel Advocate · Bathinda, Punjab

⚖ Senior Advocate 🏭 MSMED Panel 📋 LIC Panel 🏛 P&H High Court

Bharat Bhushan Singla

Senior Advocate | Bathinda, Punjab

Advocate Bharat Bhushan Singla is a distinguished Senior Advocate based in Bathinda, Punjab, with over two decades of principled legal practice across the courts of Punjab and the Punjab & Haryana High Court at Chandigarh. Enrolled with the Bar Council of Punjab & Haryana, he has built a reputation as a tenacious, principled, and deeply knowledgeable advocate.

He holds the distinction of being empanelled as an MSMED Panel Advocate — one of a select group of advocates designated to represent matters under the Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises Development Act 2006 in Punjab. This appointment reflects his specialised expertise in commercial and enterprise law as it intersects with the MSME sector, which forms the backbone of Punjab's economy.

Additionally, Advocate Singla has been appointed as an LIC Panel Advocate, giving him in-depth exposure to insurance jurisprudence, claims disputes, and policyholder rights — a niche critical to the thousands of LIC policyholders across Bathinda and the wider Malwa region of Punjab.

Beyond his specialist panel appointments, Advocate Singla maintains a broad general practice spanning civil litigation, criminal defence, consumer protection, family and matrimonial law, and property disputes. He has appeared before District Courts in Bathinda, Mansa, Muktsar, and Faridkot, as well as the Punjab & Haryana High Court at Chandigarh.

A firm believer in accessible justice, Advocate Singla is actively involved in legal awareness initiatives and pro bono representation for deserving clients in his community. He is known for his clear client communication, ethical practice, and unwavering commitment to his clients' causes.

Career Journey

Professional Milestones

2003
Enrolled at Bar Council of Punjab & Haryana

Commenced legal practice at District Courts, Bathinda. Early years focused on civil and criminal trial advocacy, building a strong procedural foundation.

2006–2010
Expanded Practice to Punjab & Haryana High Court

Began regularly appearing before the Punjab & Haryana High Court at Chandigarh in civil revision, criminal revision, and constitutional matters.

2008
MSMED Act Practice Commenced

Following the enactment of the MSMED Act 2006, developed specialist expertise in MSME payment disputes, facilitation council proceedings, and enterprise law.

2012
Appointed to LIC Panel, Punjab Region

Designated as LIC Panel Advocate, marking formal recognition of expertise in insurance law, claims disputes, and policyholder rights across Punjab.

2015
Appointed MSMED Panel Advocate

Selected as empanelled MSMED Panel Advocate by the Punjab Government, one of few advocates in Bathinda to hold this specialist designation.

2018
Recognised as Senior Advocate

Recognised within the Bathinda Bar for exceptional legal acumen and long-standing contribution to the legal profession in Punjab.

2020–Present
Continued Practice & Community Leadership

Active practice across all courts; involvement in Bar Association activities, legal aid, and mentoring junior advocates in Bathinda.
