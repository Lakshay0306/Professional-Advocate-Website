MSMED Act Cases

As an empanelled MSMED Panel Advocate in Punjab, Advocate Singla specialises in disputes under the Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises Development Act 2006. This covers payment recovery by MSMEs from buyers, MSME facilitation council proceedings, arbitration referrals, and related civil recovery matters. If your business is owed money or you are contesting an MSME claim, our expertise in this niche area is unparalleled in the Bathinda region.

LIC & Insurance Claims

As a LIC Panel Advocate, Advocate Bharat Bhushan Singla represents policyholders whose life insurance, health insurance, or general insurance claims have been repudiated, delayed, or underpaid. We handle claim appeals before the Insurance Ombudsman, IRDAI grievance proceedings, and consumer forum complaints against insurers — fighting to secure the benefits your family deserves.

Civil Litigation

From money recovery suits and injunctions to declaratory suits and specific performance of contracts, the firm's civil practice covers the full spectrum of civil disputes before District Courts Bathinda and the Punjab & Haryana High Court. We approach every civil matter with methodical preparation, evidence-based arguments, and a clear litigation strategy from day one.

Criminal Defence

Facing a criminal charge demands a fearless, experienced advocate in your corner. We represent accused individuals at every stage — bail applications, trial defence, High Court revisions, and appeals. Our criminal practice spans IPC offences, economic offences, NDPS matters, and more. Every client receives a robust, confidential, and constitutionally grounded defence strategy.

Consumer Court Cases

Deficient services, defective products, unfair trade practices — consumer grievances deserve a tenacious legal voice. We file and argue complaints before District Consumer Disputes Redressal Commissions and the State Commission, Punjab. Our consumer practice has a high rate of compensation awards and successful settlements in cases involving builders, banks, auto companies, hospitals, and e-commerce platforms.

Property & Real Estate Disputes

Property disputes in Punjab often involve complex revenue laws, ancestral property claims, partition suits, and mutation challenges. With deep knowledge of the Punjab Land Revenue Act and Transfer of Property Act, we handle contested wills, benami transactions, builder-buyer disputes, and encroachment matters before civil courts and the High Court.

Cheque Bounce Cases (Sec. 138 NI Act)

Dishonoured cheques are a common commercial grievance. We handle both complainant-side prosecution and accused-side defence in Section 138 Negotiable Instruments Act cases before Magistrate Courts in Bathinda. We manage the entire process — from filing the complaint and demand notice to final arguments — with prompt turnaround and regular client updates.

Family & Matrimonial Law
